How to use them

  • Flashcard drill — look at a Braille pattern card and try to say the letter before checking it against the letter deck.
  • Memory Match — shuffle both decks together face-down, and take turns flipping two cards to find matching pairs, just like the Memory Match game in the app.
  • Group practice — great for a classroom or family setting where more than one person is learning at once.

Print on cardstock if you have it — it holds up far better to repeated shuffling and cutting than regular paper.
